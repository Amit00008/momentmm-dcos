## Frequently Asked Questions

### For Event Organizers

#### Getting Started

**Q: How do I create my first event on Momentmm?**

A: Creating an event is simple:
1. Sign up for an organizer account at [app.momentmm.xyz/organizer](https://app.momentmm.xyz/organizer)
2. Connect your cryptocurrency wallet
3. Complete your profile verification
4. Click "Create New Event" and fill in the details
5. Configure your ticket tiers and pricing
6. Set resale rules and royalty preferences
7. Mint your NFT tickets

**Q: What are the platform fees for organizers?**

A: Our transparent fee structure:
- Primary ticket sales: 2-3% platform fee
- Secondary market transactions: 2-3% platform fee
- Payment processing: Standard card/UPI fees (covered by payment providers)
- No hidden fees or surprise charges

**Q: How do royalties work for event organizers?**

A: Organizers can set automated royalties (typically 5-10%) that are automatically distributed from every secondary market sale. This creates perpetual revenue streams:
- Set your royalty percentage during event creation
- Royalties are automatically collected and distributed
- No manual intervention required
- All transactions are transparently recorded on the blockchain

#### Event Management

**Q: Can I modify my event after it's created?**

A: Yes, you can modify certain aspects of your event:
- Event details (name, description, venue info)
- Ticket pricing and tiers (before sales begin)
- Resale rules and royalty settings
- Artwork and metadata

However, some aspects cannot be changed once tickets are sold:
- Maximum ticket quantity
- Event date and time
- Smart contract rules that affect existing ticket holders

**Q: How do I handle refunds?**

A: Refunds are handled through our standard process:
1. Contact our support team with refund requests
2. Provide event details and ticket information
3. We'll verify the request and process refunds
4. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
5. NFT tickets are burned when refunds are issued

**Q: What analytics are available for my events?**

A: Our analytics dashboard provides comprehensive insights:
- Real-time ticket sales data
- Secondary market activity
- Fan demographics and geographic distribution
- Revenue tracking (primary sales + royalties)
- Attendee verification and check-in data
- Post-event engagement metrics

### For Fans & Attendees

#### Getting Started

**Q: How do I create a wallet and buy tickets?**

A: Getting started is easy:
1. Download the Momentmm app from App Store or Google Play
2. Create an account with email or social login
3. Your digital wallet is automatically created
4. Browse events and select tickets
5. Pay with UPI, credit card, or cryptocurrency
6. Your NFT tickets are instantly transferred to your wallet

**Q: Is my wallet safe and secure?**

A: Yes, your wallet is extremely secure:
- Non-custodial: Only you control your private keys
- Biometric authentication: Fingerprint or face ID protection
- Military-grade encryption: Bank-level security
- Recovery phrases: Backup options to restore access
- Regular security audits: Ongoing security improvements

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**

A: We support multiple payment options:
- **UPI**: Instant bank transfers (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay)
- **Credit/Debit Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, RuPay
- **Cryptocurrency**: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Polygon (MATIC)
- **Digital Wallets**: All major Indian digital wallets

#### Using Tickets

**Q: How do I transfer tickets to friends?**

A: Transferring tickets is simple and secure:
1. Open the Momentmm app
2. Navigate to your ticket collection
3. Select the ticket you want to transfer
4. Enter your friend's username or wallet address
5. Add an optional personal message
6. Confirm the transfer

The ticket is instantly transferred with full ownership rights.

**Q: Can I resell my tickets?**

A: Yes, you can resell tickets on our secondary marketplace:
- List your ticket with your desired price
- Price caps prevent excessive markups
- Automatic verification ensures authenticity
- Creators earn royalties from every resale
- Fair pricing protects all fans

**Q: What happens if I lose my phone or can't access my wallet?**

A: Don't worry, you have several recovery options:
- **Recovery Phrase**: Use your 12-word backup phrase to restore access
- **Support Team**: Contact us for assistance with account recovery
- **Venue Support**: Staff can verify your ticket using your wallet address
- **Backup Access**: Keep your wallet address and recovery phrase safe

#### Technical Questions

**Q: Which blockchain do you use?**

A: We're built on Polygon for optimal performance:
- Low transaction fees (fractions of a cent)
- Fast transaction processing (2-second blocks)
- Ethereum compatibility (EVM)
- High security and decentralization

**Q: How do gasless transactions work?**

A: We use meta-transactions to eliminate gas fees for users:
- Users sign transactions with their private key
- Relayers execute transactions on the blockchain
- Platform covers all gas costs
- Seamless Web2-like user experience

**Q: Can I integrate Momentmm with my existing platform?**

A: Yes, we offer flexible integration options:
- REST API for backend integration
- SDKs for popular programming languages
- Webhook support for real-time updates
- Custom integration support for enterprise clients

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### App Issues

**Problem**: App crashes or won't open
**Solutions**:
1. Update to the latest version
2. Restart your device
3. Clear app cache and data
4. Reinstall the app if necessary
5. Contact support if issues persist

**Problem**: Can't see my tickets
**Solutions**:
1. Check your wallet connection
2. Refresh the app
3. Verify you're logged into the correct account
4. Ensure you're on the correct network (Polygon)
5. Check your internet connection

#### Payment Issues

**Problem**: Payment failed or was declined
**Solutions**:
1. Verify payment method details
2. Ensure sufficient funds are available
3. Try a different payment method
4. Contact your bank if card issues persist
5. Check for any payment restrictions

**Problem**: Transaction stuck or pending
**Solutions**:
1. Wait for network confirmation (usually 2-5 minutes)
2. Check transaction status on Polygon explorer
3. Contact support with transaction hash
4. Don't attempt duplicate transactions

#### Event Day Issues

**Problem**: QR code not working at venue
**Solutions**:
1. Refresh the app and try again
2. Check your internet connection
3. Ensure the ticket is valid for the current event
4. Contact venue staff for assistance
5. They can verify your ticket using your wallet address

**Problem**: Phone battery died or lost phone
**Solutions**:
1. Contact venue staff immediately
2. Provide your wallet address for verification
3. They can verify ticket ownership on the blockchain
4. Keep your wallet address backed up safely
